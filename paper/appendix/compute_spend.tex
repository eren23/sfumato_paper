\paragraph{Reproducibility.}
The full experimental record (training scripts, eval scripts,
LoRA adapters, derived datasets, raw per-problem JSONL outputs,
and W\&B run links) is publicly available.  All four LoRA
adapters are on the Hugging Face Hub under the \code{eren23/}
namespace:
\code{sfumato-llada-prefix-robust-v2},
\code{sfumato-llada-prefix-robust-v3},
\code{sfumato-llada-commit-v2}, and
\code{sfumato-llada-commit-v3}.
The two derived datasets are \code{eren23/sfumato-prefix-robust-gsm8k}
($59{,}784$ rows) and \code{eren23/sfumato-commit-mixture-gsm8k}
($109$ rows).
