\subsection{Track 1 (Prefix-Robustness)}

Both v2 and v3 train on the
\code{eren23/sfumato-prefix-robust-gsm8k} dataset (7,473
GSM8K-train problems crossed with $8$ prefix tiers, $59{,}784$
rows total, $95/5$ train/validation split).  The LLaDA training
objective is the standard masked-diffusion CE loss: sample
$t \sim U(0,1)$, set $p_\text{mask} = (1-\epsilon)t + \epsilon$,
mask response tokens with probability $p_\text{mask}$, replace
with \code{MASK\_ID}, and apply CE only on masked positions
re-weighted by $1/p_\text{mask}$.  Prompt tokens are never masked.

\paragraph{$p_\text{mask}$ note.}
Track 1 v1 (not reported in the body except for the v1 catastrophe
note in \appref{app:open}) used $p_\text{mask} \sim U(\epsilon,
1{-}\epsilon)$, which over-weighted very-high-mask cases at
training time.  At inference, where the diffusion sampler
typically uncovers $\sim 16\text{--}32$ fresh tokens per sub-block
at moderate $p_\text{mask}$, the v1 model defaulted to a learned
copy-back-context heuristic (bigram repetition loops).  v2 narrowed
the range to $U(0.1, 0.5)$ to match the regime \LLaDA{} actually
inhabits at inference.  v3 inherits this fix.

\subsection{Track 2 (Commit Adapter)}

Both v2 and v3 train on the
\code{eren23/sfumato-commit-mixture-gsm8k} dataset ($109$ rows,
$104$ train / $5$ validation), with three buckets: $46$ rescue,
$58$ preserve-disagreement, $5$ pure-agreement.  The trainer
re-uses the \LLaDA{} CE loss from Track 1 but masks a configurable
span: answer-only \code{[answer\_start, answer\_end)} for v2, full
response \code{[prompt\_len, response\_end)} for v3.

\paragraph{Note on Track 2 v1.}
Track 2 v1 (briefly mentioned in \S\ref{subsec:distill_plateau})
used \code{target\_modules=[gate\_proj, up\_proj, down\_proj]} ---
the standard Llama list, which only matches \code{up\_proj} on
\LLaDA{}'s actual block.  This produced a $4.2$M-parameter adapter
that attached to $1/3$ of FFN.  Discovering this bug while
debugging Track 2 led to inspecting the Track 1 modules, and to
the v3 retrain on the \LLaDA{}-correct list.  v1 results are not
reported in the body except as the matched $70.5\%$ \ctwoc{}
plateau on the v1/v2 row of Table~\ref{tab:distill_main}.

\paragraph{Inference configuration.}
All evaluations use $k{=}64$ denoising steps and
\code{gen\_length=128} tokens organized as $4$ sub-blocks of $32$.
Stochastic conditions (\code{cmaj}, \code{cmajc}) sample $b{=}5$
branches at $t{=}0.7$ and majority-vote on extracted numeric
answers.  Deterministic conditions (\ctwo{}, \ctwoc{},
\code{C2hint}, \code{C2empty}, \cthree{}\code{p X}) use $t{=}0$
greedy decoding.
